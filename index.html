About Us / 關於我們

Who We Are 我們是誰

Our Mission

Taiwan Q-Fin Hub is an initiative connecting Taiwanese youth with the forefront of quantum computing and quantitative finance. We partner with leading US research universities and industry organizations to give participants hands-on experience in hybrid quantum-classical computing (HQCC), AI-driven financial engineering, and international capital markets.

Our flagship overseas program is supported by Taiwan's Ministry of Education Youth Development Agency, under the Youth Overseas Dream Fund.

我們的使命

台灣量子金融實驗室致力於將台灣青年與量子計算和量化金融的最前沿相連結。我們與美國頂尖研究大學及產業組織合作，讓參與者在混合量子古典計算（HQCC）、AI驅動金融工程及國際資本市場獲得實戰經驗。

本計畫旗艦海外項目由教育部青年發展署「青年百億海外圓夢基金」支持。

Program / 計畫內容

Quantum Finance US Internship 量子金融：赴美職涯領航

A 35-day immersive program in the Greater New York area (July 6 – August 9), combining lab visits, expert seminars, and industry networking at three world-class universities.

為期35天的大紐約區沉浸式計畫（7月6日至8月9日），結合實驗室參訪、專家講座與三所世界頂尖大學的業界交流。

⚛️

HQCC Research Labs量子混合計算實驗室

Hands-on visits to Hybrid Quantum-Classical Computing labs at NJIT and Stony Brook University.

親訪NJIT與石溪大學的HQCC實驗室，體驗量子古典混合計算前沿研究。

📈

Financial Engineering Seminars金融工程專題講座

Expert-led sessions on quantum algorithms applied to portfolio optimization, risk modeling, and AI-driven trading.

量子演算法應用於投資組合優化、風險建模及AI驅動交易的專家主講課程。

Industry Networking產業專家座談

Direct access to NYC investment industry leaders and Taiwanese-American tech professionals via TAITA East.

透過TAITA美東分會，直接接觸紐約投資業領袖及台美科技專業人士。

跨校Quantum AI與AI資料中心應用講座，涵蓋NJIT、SUNY與福坦謨大學。

福坦謨大學曼哈頓與布朗克斯校區提供跨領域金融、AI及國際研究課程。

Eligibility / 申請資格

Who Can Apply 申請條件

✅ Required 必要條件

  • ROC nationality, aged 18–30
  • Full participation in all program activities (including pre-departure training, info sessions, boot camp, and results presentation)
  • Own professional equipment (laptop, smartphone) and presentable attire
  • Submit final report and program video
  • 具中華民國國籍，18–30歲之青年
  • 須全程參與計畫行程（含行前培訓、說明會、集訓及成果發表會）
  • 自備筆記型電腦、智慧型手機及整齊服裝
  • 依規繳交成果報告與成果影片

⭐ Preferred 優先條件

  • English proficiency equivalent to CEFR B1 or above; strong communication skills preferred
  • Background in technology-related fields: STEM, management, or finance/quantitative disciplines
  • Strong motivation for quantum finance and US industry exposure
  • 英文能力達CEFR B1以上，能進行基礎對話；良好溝通能力者尤佳
  • 具備科技產業相關領域背景（理工、管理 / 金融）
  • 對量子金融及美國業界有強烈學習動機
