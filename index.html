About the Program

Taiwan Q-Fin Hub Summer 2026

Taiwan Q-Fin Hub is a student cohort participating in Taiwan's Youth Overseas Dream Fund, supported by the Youth Development Administration, Ministry of Education.

In summer 2026, the cohort will visit the Greater New York area for an academic and professional exchange program focused on quantum computing, artificial intelligence, financial technology, and quantitative finance.

This website serves as a public profile of the cohort and the summer program. It helps professors, university staff, industry professionals, and community partners understand what the students are working on, while also giving participants a credible page to link to their learning experience on LinkedIn and other professional profiles.

Through academic visits, expert discussions, professional networking, and post-program reflection, students will explore how emerging technologies connect with financial systems, research communities, and global innovation ecosystems.

Program

Quantum Finance US Internship

A 35-day immersive program in the Greater New York area (July 6 - August 9), combining lab visits, expert seminars, and industry networking across university research and finance communities.

Quantum Computing & AI Research Exposure

Exposure to hybrid quantum-classical computing, quantum algorithms, and AI research through academic visits, lab discussions, and technical briefings.

Financial Engineering Seminars

Seminars on financial engineering, quantitative finance, and AI in finance, including risk modeling, portfolio methods, and market applications.

Professional Networking

Professional exchange with industry practitioners, Taiwanese-American technology leaders, and community partners through TAITA East and related networks.

Learning Outcomes

Participants develop reports, presentations, and follow-up sharing that reflect what they learned from research exposure, professional discussions, and site visits.

Schedule

5-Week Itinerary

Week 1

Greater New York Research Orientation

  • Campus and research environment orientation
  • Financial Engineering & HQCC Lab visits (2 sessions)
  • HQCC thematic lectures and research exchanges (2 sessions)
  • NYC investment industry practice & expert roundtables (2 sessions)
Week 2

HQCC and Financial AI Workshop Week

  • In-depth HQCC × Finance thematic workshops (2 sessions)
  • Seminar with Financial AI researchers and professors
  • Group discussion and learning synthesis (2 sessions)
  • NYC investment industry practice & expert panels (2 sessions)
Week 3

Research and Industry Exchange

  • HQCC & Quantum AI technology lectures (2 sessions)
  • Advanced research training + industry/culture introduction (2 sessions)
  • Cross-campus student & faculty exchange activities (2 sessions)
  • NYC investment industry roundtable
Week 4

Urban Finance and AI Residency

  • Urban university interdisciplinary lectures (2 sessions)
  • HQCC & AI Data Center applications symposium (2 sessions)
  • Industry expert visits arranged by international finance mentors (2 sessions)
  • NYC investment practice & expert roundtable
Week 5

Synthesis & Wrap-up

  • Individual results compilation and sharing (2 sessions)
  • Final group debriefs (2 sessions)
  • Post-program networking and follow-up connections
  • Return flight to Taiwan
